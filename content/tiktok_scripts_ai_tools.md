## FILMING TIPS

1. **First 1-2 seconds are everything** - Hook must stop the scroll
2. **Show, don't just tell** - Screen recordings perform better
3. **Talk like a friend** - Not salesy, just sharing what works
4. **Add captions** - 80% watch without sound
5. **End with soft CTA** - "Link in bio" not "BUY NOW"
6. **Post 1-3x daily** - Volume wins on TikTok
7. **Reply to every comment** - Algorithm loves engagement
8. **Use trending sounds** - When relevant
