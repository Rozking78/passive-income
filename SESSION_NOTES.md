## Content Strategy

### Platform Priority
1. **TikTok** - Low effort, viral potential, quick to test
2. **Instagram Reels** - Cross-post TikTok content
3. **YouTube Shorts** - Cross-post TikTok content
4. **Twitter/X** - Threads for authority
5. **Blog** - SEO long game

### Posting Schedule
| Platform | Frequency | Best Times |
|----------|-----------|------------|
| TikTok | 1-3x daily | 7am, 12pm, 7pm |
| Reels | 1x daily | Same content as TikTok |
| Twitter | 1 thread/week | Tue-Thu mornings |
| Blog | 2 posts/week | Evergreen SEO |

### Content Types That Convert
1. **Hook videos** - "This AI tool is breaking the internet"
2. **Tutorials** - "How to create a week of content in 10 min"
3. **Results/Proof** - "What happened after 30 days"
4. **Comparisons** - "Jasper vs Copy.ai"
5. **Storytimes** - "I almost quit until I found this"

## Mindset Notes

### What Works
- **Consistency > Perfection** - Post daily, improve over time
- **Volume wins** - More content = more chances to go viral
- **Soft sells** - "Link in bio" not "BUY NOW"
- **Value first** - Teach something, then mention tool
- **Authenticity** - Share real results and honest reviews

### What Doesn't Work
- Posting once a week and expecting results
- Hard selling in every video
- Copying viral videos word-for-word
- Giving up after 2 weeks
- Not tracking what works
